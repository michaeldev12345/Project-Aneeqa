💌 Meri Pyari Choti Behen, Aneeqa ❤️

Happy Birthday! 🎉🎂

Kabhi kabhi zindagi humein aise logon se mila deti hai jinse milne ka humne kabhi socha bhi nahi hota. 2024 mein Instagram par tumse ek choti si baat shuru hui thi, aur us waqt bilkul bhi andaza nahi tha ke ek din tum meri choti behen ban jaogi.

Har rishta khoon ka nahi hota, kuch rishte Allah khud banata hai. Tum bhi meri zindagi ka aisa hi ek khoobsurat rishta ho, aur main Allah ka shukar ada karta hoon ke usne mujhe tum jaisi pyari behen di.

Aaj tumhare birthday par meri dil se yehi dua hai ke Allah tumhe hamesha khush rakhe, sehatmand rakhe, tumhari har jaiz dua qubool kare, aur tumhari zindagi ko sukoon, barkat aur kamyabi se bhar de. Ameen. 🤲

Hamesha muskurati rehna, apne sapnon ke liye mehnat karna, aur kabhi apne Rab par bharosa mat khona.

In Sha Allah tumhari zindagi ka har naya saal pichle saal se bhi zyada khoobsurat, khushiyon se bhara aur yaadgaar hoga.

Aur ek baat... ❤️

Chahe zindagi humein kitna hi busy kyun na kar de, ya waqt kitna hi badal kyun na jaye, tum hamesha meri choti behen rahogi.

Allah kare hamara ye pyara sa rishta hamesha isi tarah salamat rahe.

Happy Birthday once again, meri pyari choti behen! ❤️🎉

May Allah always protect you, guide you, and fill your life with endless happiness.

— Tumhara Bada Bhai 💙
